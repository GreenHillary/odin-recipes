Quinoa Salad

Description

This classic quinoa salad recipe is a quick and tasty mediterranean meal the whole family will enjoy. Mix it up by adding your favorite crunchy topping!

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 cup feta cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 lemons
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped fresh spinach
  1. Cook quinoa according to package and allow to cool
  2. Chop onion and cucumber
  3. Chop garlic and whisk with olive oil and lemon juice
  4. Mix quinoa, feta, veggies, and dressing in a large bowl
  5. Let mixture marinate for 5 minutes, then enjoy!

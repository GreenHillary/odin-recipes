Description

Everyone loves a good hamburger! This recipe is for a basic classic burger, but you can customize it with any toppings you like.

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 package buns
  • salt and pepper to taste
  1. Add salt and pepper to raw ground beef, mix well
  2. Shape beef into patties
  3. Grill patties
  4. Add your favorite sauces and veggies to buns
  5. Assemble and enjoy!

Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is easy and delicious, making it perfect for anyone who loves comfort food. Adults and kids alike will be asking for seconds!

  • 1 box lasagna noodles
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 cups veggies
  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package
  2. Mix ricotta and mozzarella
  3. Cook veggies with garlic
  4. Layer baking dish with noodles, cheese, sauce, and veggies
  5. Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes or until cheese is bubbly
